Starting with a 3-Month Supply

Building a full year's supply can feel overwhelming. The key is to start small and build progressively. Here's the approach:

  1. Know your family's weekly food needs — think about what you actually cook and eat in a normal week.
  2. Multiply by 7 to find your one-week supply, then buy a little extra each shopping trip to build your stock.
  3. Expand gradually: week → month → three months → one year. Each milestone is a real achievement.
  4. Rotate regularly — eat what you store and replace it. This keeps quality high and prevents waste.
Quick math: Take any annual total from the calculator above, divide by 4 for a 3-month goal, or by 12 for a monthly purchase target.
